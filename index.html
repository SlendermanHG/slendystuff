A Word From the Dungeon Master

“Before there was a bot, before there was code — there was just a table, a notebook, and a few friends lost in the story.”

The Chronicle was born from that place. From long nights spent trying to recall the name of a barkeep, or where a relic was last seen. I built this because I’ve been there — because I’m a Dungeon Master too.

I’ve juggled notes, worried about continuity, and wished for something that could remember the world so I could focus on *playing it*. The Chronicle is that wish made real — a companion for every DM who’s ever wanted to keep their stories whole.

This isn’t corporate or commercial — it’s personal. Every command, every line of code, every small improvement comes from nights spent building while dice cooled on the table beside me. The Chronicle was crafted from passion, not profit.

If you take nothing else from this, remember: you are not alone. There’s an entire world of storytellers who understand the joy and struggle of shaping worlds from imagination. This project is for them — for *us.*

So if you ever have questions, thoughts, or tales to share, reach out. I read every message, because every DM’s story matters.
