About Slendy Stuff

Every DM knows the struggle — notes scattered across notebooks, files, or messages lost between sessions. We built the Chronicle because stories deserve better. Slendy Stuff began as a simple idea: what if a bot could remember everything the party learns — faithfully, quietly, and without judgment? Now, DMs of every level can focus on what they love most: crafting worlds, weaving tales, and watching their players shape the story — knowing that every detail, every secret, and every victory is being preserved.
