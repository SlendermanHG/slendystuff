About Me, Not A Pitch

I'm Slendy (SlendermanHG).

I live in the USA. I work a full-time job, and this project is what I build during focused off-hours. A lot of this started from ADHD-level obsession with solving weird tech pain points that most people ignore.

10+ years building Full-time job + side studio Still maintaining older builds Always building something new

Why slendystuff exists

The name came from my long-time handle and nickname. The mission came from real frustration: seeing people lose time and confidence because tech felt harder than it should. This is where I channel that energy into practical fixes and tools that feel human to use.

"If tech feels heavy, I want to make it lighter."
